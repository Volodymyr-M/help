What You Can Do with Ingantt #

Ingantt is a project scheduling and tracking app. You enter your project's data — tasks, durations, dependencies, resources — and Ingantt gives you answers.

When planning the project #

  • When will it finish? — calculated automatically from your task dependencies, not guessed.
  • Which resources are needed? — what people, equipment, and materials each task requires.
  • Who is overloaded? — which resources have more work than they can handle and when.
  • What will it cost? — total project cost based on resource rates and fixed costs.
  • Which tasks matter most? — the critical path shows where a delay will push out the entire project.

Once work is underway #

  • Are we on track? — compare current schedule progress against the original plan.
  • Are we on budget? — compare actual costs against planned costs using Earned Value metrics.
  • How far along are we really? — overall project % complete, weighted by task duration, not gut feeling.
  • What has been spent and what remains? — actual vs. remaining cost, work, and duration.

Change one task's duration or add a dependency, and the entire schedule recalculates instantly — every date, every resource workload, every cost.
